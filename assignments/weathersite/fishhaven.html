This is Fish Heaven

by Les Nessman

Fish Haven is a community along the shores of Bear Lake in Bear Lake County, Idaho. The first settlement at Fish Haven was made in 1864. A post office called Fish Haven was established in 1867, and remained in operation until 1962. The community was so named because nearby Bear Lake is a favorite fishing spot
